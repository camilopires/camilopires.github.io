Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my portfolio website (camilopires.co.uk). I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information I Collect

When you visit my website, I may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and message content when you use the contact form
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the website, including pages visited and time spent
  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device information
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Analyse website usage and performance
  • Send important updates about my services (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. I may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect my rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in website operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications

7. Cookies

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) that collect information about your usage. These services have their own privacy policies, and I encourage you to review them.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or my data practises, please contact me at:

Email: privacy@camilopires.co.uk
Website: camilopires.co.uk

11. BoxingScorecard App Privacy Policy

This section outlines the privacy policy specifically for the BoxingScorecard mobile application.

1. Information We Collect (BoxingScorecard)

BoxingScorecard collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile information when you create an account
  • Match Data: Boxing match scores, fighter information, and match statistics that you input
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support

2. How We Use Your Information (BoxingScorecard)

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the BoxingScorecard service
  • Process and store your match scoring data
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications about the app
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Analyse usage patterns to enhance app features

3. Data Storage and Security (BoxingScorecard)

Your data security is our priority:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Your match data is stored securely on our servers
  • We regularly review and update our security practises

4. Data Sharing (BoxingScorecard)

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in app operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights (BoxingScorecard)

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your match data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

6. Data Retention (BoxingScorecard)

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, though some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

7. Children's Privacy (BoxingScorecard)

BoxingScorecard is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy (BoxingScorecard)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us (BoxingScorecard)

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practises, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@boxingscorecard.com
