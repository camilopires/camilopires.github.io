Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: September 2, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my portfolio website (camilopires.co.uk). I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information I Collect

When you visit my website, I may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and message content when you use the contact form
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the website, including pages visited and time spent
  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device information
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Analyse website usage and performance
  • Send important updates about my services (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. I may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect my rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in website operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications

7. Cookies

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) that collect information about your usage. These services have their own privacy policies, and I encourage you to review them.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or my data practises, please contact me at:

Email: dev@camilopires.co.uk
Website: camilopires.co.uk
