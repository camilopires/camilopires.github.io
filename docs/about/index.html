About Me

Product Manager with an accessibility-first mindset and iOS development expertise, crafting inclusive digital experiences that drive revenue and empower users

My Background

Hey! I'm a Product Manager who loves putting customers first and making things that actually work. I've been lucky enough to work on products from the initial "what if we..." moment all the way through to launch, using my technical background to bridge the gap between business goals and what's actually possible to build.

I'm all about making smart decisions based on data and real user feedback, not just gut feelings. I've helped companies make some serious revenue through solid product strategies, and I've pitched business cases to some pretty senior folks at Fortune 500 companies.

As a disability advocate and wheelchair user myself, I'm passionate about making sure digital products work for everyone. I bring accessibility into every product decision because I believe everyone deserves amazing digital experiences because equity matters. Always.
