About Me

Product Manager with a customer-focused lens, generating significant revenue through data-driven strategies and advocating for accessibility across Fortune 500 companies

My Background

I'm an established Product Manager with a customer-focused lens, aligned to business needs and scope. My journey spans full product lifecycle management from idea conception to launch, using technical programming and design knowledge to be the ultimate bridge between business, design and IT functions across multiple timezones and geographies.

Process-driven, decisive and energetic in approach, I make decisions using research, data and past experience to create analytically-sound conclusions. I've generated significant revenue through data-driven product strategies and have experience producing business cases for top SVPs in Fortune 500 companies.

As a disability advocate and accessibility consultant, improving diversity and becoming an advocate is of top importance and is brought into all product decisions and company culture. I'm passionate about bringing disability to the forefront of conversations whilst breaking records and stereotypes.
