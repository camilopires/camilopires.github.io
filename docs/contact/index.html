Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in mobile app development, particularly React Native applications, and product management. I work on projects ranging from sports applications like BoxingScorecard to business tools and consumer apps.

Do you work remotely?

Yes, I work remotely and am available for projects worldwide. I'm comfortable with various communication tools and time zone coordination.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical mobile app project can take 3-6 months from concept to launch, while smaller features or consulting projects may take weeks.

How do you handle project communication?

I believe in transparent and regular communication. I provide weekly updates, use project management tools, and am always available for questions and feedback throughout the process.
