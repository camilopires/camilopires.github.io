The Challenge & Solution

Career growth requires consistent effort‚Äîpreparing for 1:1 meetings, tracking action items, setting meaningful goals, and documenting achievements for performance reviews. Most professionals struggle to maintain this discipline with scattered notes, forgotten follow-ups, and missing context when review season arrives.

Career Companion transforms career management into a seamless experience. Built with SwiftUI and CloudKit, it provides a secure, private space to prepare agendas, capture meeting notes, track action items, and build a comprehensive record of your professional achievements‚Äîall synced across your Apple devices.

Whether you're an individual contributor managing 1:1s with your manager, or a manager tracking meetings with multiple direct reports, Career Companion adapts to your workflow. With AI-powered suggestions, Siri integration, and an Apple Watch companion app, your career data is always at your fingertips.
