Privacy Policy for Boxing Scorecard

How we collect, use, and safeguard your information

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

Introduction

Boxing Scorecard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Fight Data: Boxing match information including boxer names, scores, rounds, and match details
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality
  • iCloud Data: Fight data synced through Apple's iCloud service

How We Collect Information

  • User Input: Information you enter when creating and scoring fights
  • iCloud Sync: Automatic synchronization through Apple's CloudKit service
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: To provide boxing scorecard services
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your fight data across your devices via iCloud
  • App Improvement: To enhance app performance and user experience

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Fight data is stored locally on your device using Core Data
  • iCloud Storage: Fight data is synced to your personal iCloud account
  • No Third-Party Storage: We do not store your data on our servers

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Share Your Data

  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your fight data with other users or external services
  • Your data remains private and accessible only to you

Apple Services

  • iCloud: Your fight data syncs through Apple's iCloud service
  • App Store: Basic app analytics provided by Apple
  • Device Services: Standard iOS device integration

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using Apple's standard encryption
  • iCloud Security: Data synced through iCloud benefits from Apple's security measures
  • Local Security: Device-level security protects local data

Your Responsibilities

  • Keep your device and iCloud account secure
  • Use strong passwords for your Apple ID
  • Enable two-factor authentication for additional security

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All fight data is visible within the app
  • Delete Data: You can delete individual fights or all data through the app
  • iCloud Control: Manage iCloud sync through your device settings

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable iCloud Sync: Turn off iCloud sync in device settings
  • Delete App: Removing the app deletes local data
  • iCloud Data: Manage iCloud data through Apple's iCloud settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

If you are using our app outside the United Kingdom, please note that your information may be transferred to and stored in the United Kingdom or other countries where our service providers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: dev@camilopires.co.uk
Developer: Camilo Pires

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • App Store Guidelines: Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (for EU users, including UK)
  • UK Data Protection Act 2018: UK data protection legislation

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete it or remove the app
  • iCloud Data: Follows Apple's iCloud data retention policies
  • Backup Data: Subject to your device's backup settings

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of the Boxing Scorecard app.
