BoxingScorecard

A professional iOS mobile application for scoring boxing matches with real-time updates and comprehensive statistics, designed for both enthusiasts and professionals.

The Challenge & Solution

The world of boxing needed a modern, reliable, and user-friendly platform for scoring matches. Traditional methods were often inconsistent or lacked the depth required by serious fans and judges. BoxingScorecard was developed to fill this gap, offering a robust solution that brings professional-level scoring to everyone's fingertips.

My goal was to create an intuitive app that not only allowed users to score rounds in real-time but also provided detailed statistics, fighter profiles, and a seamless cross-device experience. This involved intricate UI/UX design, real-time data synchronization, and a focus on performance to ensure a smooth user experience even during live events.
